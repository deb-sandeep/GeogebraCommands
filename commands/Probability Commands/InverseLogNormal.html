InverseLogNormal Command

InverseLogNormal( <Mean>, <Standard Deviation>, <Probability> )

Computes the inverse of the cumulative distribution function of a log-normal distribution at probability p, where the log-normal distribution is defined by the given parameters mean and standard deviation. In other words, it finds t such that P(X â‰¤ t) = p, where X is a log-normal random variable. Probability p must be a value in the closed interval [0, 1].
